Our History and Outreach

We are thrilled to announce we are just completing our Psalm 23 Affirmation in **Zimbabwe, Africa**. This is part of a legacy of spreading this message, which includes:

  • The same contest held in **Kisumu, Kenya, in 2007**.
  • Presenting the Psalm 23 Affirmation to **Divinity Students in 2005**.
  • Using it in **Baltimore** as a call and response to children as young as 6 years old at what was previously Eutaw Marshburn Elementary School.

Benefits of the Psalm 23 Affirmation

Daily reading of the Psalm 23 Affirmation can have a profound impact on your life, helping you to:

  • Strengthen your sense of love for yourself and for others.
  • Develop a better memory and increase your brain power through a process called neuroplasticity.
  • Overcome traumatic memories resulting from abuse.
  • Break the cycle of generational transference of unhealthy thoughts, feelings, words, and actions.

Reading this affirmation regularly assists individuals and communities in developing a healthier mindset and a deeper connection to God's presence.
